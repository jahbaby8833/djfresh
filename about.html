How We Started

It has been a long and stressful journey but thanks to the Almighty God who has been faithful to me. My passion for music made me chase a career as a disk jockey and I am enjoying every part of it.

One day I was hanging out with a friend at a bar and the DJ failed to show up. My friend sugessted to the manager of the bar that I become his DJ for the day. I reluctantly agreed but after my performance, I saw that people were happy and wanted more. I asked myself, if I can make this people happy then why hide my talent? Since then it has been none stop music.

My Philosophy

My philosophy is simple, make the client happy and the guest entertained. Music is the best food we can give to ourselves. It has the ability to change our mood at anytime. Good music never dies and it is my job to make it sound pleasing to your ear.

When I am playing, I tends to remember that everybody has their different problems in their lives. Hitting them with the right track that make them forget their problem is the ultimate goal...
